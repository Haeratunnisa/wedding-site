Kisah di Balik Hari Bahagia Kami

           

                We never imagined that a simple connection would bring us this far to the moment where we choose to walk together, forever. Our story began on Facebook back when he was in the middle school, and I was in high school. Even at that young age, he made a quiet but sincere commitment to me. Time passed, and life took us in different directions. On August 24th, 2025, he returned not just with words, but with the courage to fulfill the promise he made years ago. On August 27th, we officially got engagement, surrounded by those we love. And now, we look forward to beginning our new chapter as husband and wife on September 4th, 2025, the day of our akad. This journey filled with time, distance, and unshaken intention has shaped our story. And now, we’re ready to write the rest of it, together.
